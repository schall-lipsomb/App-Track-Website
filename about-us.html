About Us

AppTrack College Solutions has helped students and parents navigate the College Application Process for over 30 years. Our team of former admissions officers and consultants understands the ins-and-outs of the college process and has a proven track record of successful outcomes.

AppTrack was founded with one goal in mind: to make education more accessible to all. Our guides, tools, workshops, and consultations have turned the College Process from a chore to a joy for thousands of families.
